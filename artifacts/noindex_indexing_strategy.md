## Classification Approach

Safe-to-index-now tools are low-risk utilities, generic planning tools, harmless randomizers, or non-brand creative tools with premium output and no obvious legal, IP, identity, platform-abuse, security, or harassment risk.

Cleanup-before-index tools are potentially useful pages where the core task can be safe, but the indexed page should first make the limits explicit: no affiliation, no fake engagement, no impersonation, no policy evasion, no fake marketplace claims, no guaranteed platform performance, no unsafe prompt instructions, and no legal sufficiency claims.

Must-remain-noindex tools include protected franchise/brand/IP surfaces, fake identity or fake document utilities, legal templates with continuing legal-advice risk, security-sensitive generators, threat/harassment-adjacent tools, and tools with meaningful deception or platform-abuse risk.
