# Editorial & EEAT Content Standards

This document defines trust signals, verification lists, and terminology guidelines.

---

## 1. Trustworthy Wording (EEAT)
- **Data safety & Privacy**: Highlight 100% browser-based calculations.
- **Cost Transparency**: Explicitly call out that the utilities are free and require no email/registration.
- **Authenticity & Non-Repetitive wording**: Avoid standard AI filler patterns or excessive generic boilerplate.
